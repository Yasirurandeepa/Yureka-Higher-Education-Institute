◈ About Yureka Institute

Yureka Educational institute stands today as one of the most success education provider in Galle district for nearly 10 years. We always giving our contribution toward education to uplift the knowledge of students and also to promote the rich culture of education despite being a profit oriented institute. We are currently having all the major courses focusing for Advanced Level and Ordinary Level examinations and also we are having brilliant and qualified panel of teachers who gives their best for the knowledge of students.

We are also providing good facilities for the students to make a better learning environment and concentrating on quality of teaching methods. Our aim is to develop knowledge, skills and attitude of our students by providing required educational background. As a result of that, currently we have over 1000 students who have joined to be a part of our family to get knowledge and to be winners. By providing quality services and good teaching methods with the support of our committed staff we are always willing to shape the knowledge of our students.

◈ Our Mission

Provide students with quality education and guide them towards the success to reach their highest potential.

◈ Our Vision

To be a leader in preparing qualified and exemplary educated students to the society.
